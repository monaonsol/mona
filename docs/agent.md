MONA AI

MONA AI is an experimental autonomous intelligence designed to observe global markets, technology trends, and major world events while interacting with the community in real time.

Inspired by the mysterious cat that watched the Renaissance unfold, MONA AI analyzes developments across crypto, macroeconomics, and global markets to provide insights and observations.

The agent will interact with users on X (Twitter), respond to community questions, and monitor market and world data in real time as the system continues to evolve.
